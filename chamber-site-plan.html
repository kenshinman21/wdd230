Site Purpose

The Purpose of my website is to make a chamber of commerce website that will attract local business in Nacogdoches. In order to make the purpose of this website come to fruition our company must increase website traffic by at least 50% and we as a community must increase the enrollment amount to at least 5 entities. The goal of increasing website traffic could be measured by using ethical and trustworthy resources like Google Analytics tools. I think all these goals could be done by at least a months time if we all work together. I could measure the increase in membership enrollment by using conversion tracking. The target audience for this website is for any small local business in Nacogdoches.

Color Schema

The reason I chose the color scheme Green and brown is with a simple line of logic. Knowing that Nacogdoches is a small town that values strong conservative values like family and strength and taking the setting of this small town. Nacogdoches is located in a forrested area despite the city's infrastructure and new construction projects. Taking all that information into account as well as a little about color theory it seemed only reasonable that Green and Brown should be the color choices.

Typography

The reason for my given choice of Typography can be summed up as some assumptions as well as my life exerpience in this rural city. I know that the community values honesty, professionalism, and tradition so it make sense to use Serif type fonts for the titles or bold words and Sans-Serif for everything else.
